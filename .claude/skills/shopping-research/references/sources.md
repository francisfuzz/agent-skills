# Source Tiers by Category

Load this file when selecting which retailers to search in Step 2.

---

## Always Check (Every Item)
- **Amazon** — note if sold by Amazon vs. third-party
- **Walmart.com**
- **Target.com**
- **Brand's official site** (if brand is known or specified)

---

## Clothing & Apparel
- Nordstrom
- Macy's
- Zappos (especially footwear)
- ASOS
- Poshmark ← secondhand, often significantly cheaper
- ThredUp ← secondhand, especially good for everyday brands

## Household Goods
- Wayfair (furniture, home décor, bedding)
- Home Depot / Lowe's (tools, hardware, appliances)
- IKEA (furniture, storage)
- Overstock / Bed Bath & Beyond
- Costco (check if membership price is worth it)

## General / Mixed
- Google Shopping ← good for surfacing lesser-known retailers
- Shop.app ← aggregates deals and cashback offers
- Honey / Capital One Shopping ← check for coupon codes at checkout

---

## Source Quality Notes
- **Marketplace sellers on Amazon/Walmart**: prefer "Sold by [Retailer]" over third-party when prices are close
- **Temu / AliExpress / Shein**: flag as direct-import risk — load `tariff-flags.md` before including
- **Facebook Marketplace / OfferUp**: only include if user explicitly wants local/secondhand options
