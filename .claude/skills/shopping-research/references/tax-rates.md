Use as a starting point. Always verify current rates — they change.
Search: "[state] sales tax rate [year] clothing household goods"

## Notes on Shipping Taxability
- Most states: tax applies to product price only, not shipping
- Exceptions (tax shipping too): Alabama, California, Colorado, Georgia, and others
- When in doubt, search: "[state] does sales tax apply to shipping charges"

---

## How to Apply
1. Find destination state from ZIP code
2. Use rate above as estimate, or search for precise local rate
3. Multiply: `product price × tax rate = tax amount`
4. Note in output if clothing exemption may apply
